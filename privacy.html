Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Content Factory ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares personal information when creators ("you", "creator") use our content scheduling platform ("Service"). The Service is hosted in Taiwan.

If you have any questions or concerns about our policy, please contact us at oblivion5438@gmail.com.

1. Information We Collect

The Service is a content scheduling platform intended for use by content creators who manage their own TikTok accounts. We do not collect personal information beyond what is necessary to operate the Service.

We do not collect:

  • Names, addresses, or contact details
  • Payment or financial information
  • Sensitive personal information (race, religion, health, etc.)
  • Geolocation data
  • Mobile device features (contacts, calendar, Bluetooth)
  • Mobile device identifiers
  • Information from third-party social media sign-in

2. Information from Third-Party APIs (TikTok)

To provide the publishing functionality, creators may authorize this platform to access their TikTok account through TikTok's official OAuth 2.0 flow. When a creator does so, we receive and store the following data on the creator's local device:

  • open_id — a TikTok-issued user identifier (not the TikTok username)
  • access_token — a short-lived credential used to call TikTok APIs (typically valid for 24 hours)
  • refresh_token — a longer-lived credential used to renew the access_token (typically valid for 365 days)
  • scope — the list of permissions granted (user.info.basic, video.upload, video.publish)

We also send the following content to TikTok on the creator's behalf when they publish a video:

  • Video files the creator has prepared
  • Post metadata (title, description, privacy setting, interaction settings) configured by the creator

We do NOT collect:

  • TikTok usernames, passwords, or any other login credentials
  • Creator's TikTok followers, following list, or social graph
  • Creator's TikTok video history beyond the videos published through the Service
  • Direct messages, comments, or notifications

3. How We Use Information

We use information solely to provide and facilitate the delivery of the Service to creators. We do not:

  • Send marketing or promotional messages
  • Use information for advertising or targeted ads
  • Sell or share information with third parties
  • Use information for AI training or automated decision-making

4. Sharing of Information

We do not share creator data with any third parties. We do not use Google Analytics or similar tracking services.

5. Cookies and Tracking

The Service does not use cookies, web beacons, or similar tracking technologies. We do not use Google Maps API or any other location tracking technologies.

6. Data Security

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. All authentication tokens are stored locally on the creator's own device, and are never transmitted to or stored on any server operated by us.

However, despite our safeguards, no electronic transmission over the Internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

7. Data Retention, Deletion, and Privacy Rights

Storage location and duration

All authentication tokens (access_token, refresh_token, open_id) are stored locally on the creator's device, in the file tokens/tiktok_token.json. They are never transmitted to or stored on any server operated by us.

Tokens persist on the local device until one of the following occurs:

  • The creator deletes them manually from the local file
  • The creator revokes authorization from TikTok account settings (Settings > Security & Privacy > Manage app permissions)
  • The refresh_token expires (approximately 365 days after issue)

Creator rights

Creators have the right to:

  • Revoke this app's access to their TikTok account at any time through TikTok's settings page
  • Delete all locally stored tokens by removing the tokens/ directory in the Service installation
  • Request information about, correction of, or deletion of any personal data, by contacting us at the email below

We will respond to such requests in accordance with applicable law, including Taiwan's Personal Data Protection Act (個人資料保護法).

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for use by children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most current version will always be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
